# Privacy Policy for Football Spades

**Last Updated: March 2026**

## Data Collection
Football Spades does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- No data is sent to external servers
- No analytics or tracking services are used

This app does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data.

## Children's Privacy
This app is safe for all ages and collects no personal information from anyone.

## Changes to This Policy
Any changes will be reflected in the app store listing.

## Contact
