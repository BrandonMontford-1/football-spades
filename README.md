# 🏈 Football Spades - Coach's Edition

A football-themed Spades card game where strategy meets the gridiron. Call your touchdowns, avoid penalties, and become a Super Bowl champion!

## 🎮 Features

- **Classic Spades Gameplay** with football-themed scoring
- **12 Unique Coaches** with distinct personalities
- **3 Difficulty Levels** - Easy, Medium, Expert
- **Premium UI** - Leather and gold design
- **Career Stats Tracking** - Win/loss records, streaks
- **Score History** - Track your last 10 rounds
- **Haptic Feedback** - Feel every play

## 🏆 Scoring System

| Action | Points | Football Term |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| Win a trick | +7 | TOUCHDOWN! |
| Make your bid | No adjustment | Two-point conversion |
| Overtrick | +3 each | FIELD GOAL |
| Missed bid | -7 per trick | OFFSIDES penalty |
| Successful shutout (bid 0) | +14 | SHUTOUT bonus |
| Failed shutout | -14 | BLOWN COVERAGE |
| 10 bags penalty | -14 | PERSONAL FOUL |
| Win game | First to 70 | SUPER BOWL! |
